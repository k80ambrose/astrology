Why I Made This

Astrology is a symbolic language. Knowledge of these symbols and what they represent is often considered a prerequisite to practicing astrology. The information above could be represented by a natal chart, but this representation is illegible to those unacquainted with Astrology’s symbolic vocabulary. Recognizing this barrier, I created this site to bridge the gap between seasoned astrologers and any individual interested in keeping up to date with planetary transits.
